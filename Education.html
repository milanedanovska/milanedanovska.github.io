My education:
I started my schooling at Kiril Pejčinovič primary school in Skopje, Kisela Voda. Then I enrolled in the high school Josip Broz-Tito, where I studied in the special German class of the natural mathematics major. During my schooling, I acquired knowledge in the field of mathematics, informatics and the German language, which have always been a field of my interest. In the fourth year, I received a certificate for B2/C1 level in German. After finishing high school, I decided to continue my studies at the Faculty of Information Sciences and Computer Engineering. I have now completed my first year here, and I am looking forward to learning many new things in this field during my studies.
